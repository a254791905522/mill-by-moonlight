About Mill By Moonlight

Mill By Moonlight is an offline lunar tide milling simulation built with SpriteKit. You operate a historic tide mill at Eling, Hampshire, where the moon drives the tides and the tides drive the water wheel that turns the millstone.

Grind wheat into flour, sell it for gold, and upgrade your equipment across 50 contracts. Each contract challenges you to reach a flour or gold target within a limited number of game days, while the moon phase cycles and the tide rises and falls.

Read the moon, wait for the spring tide, engage the millstone at the right moment, and master the rhythm of the tide mill.

How to Play

1

Read the Moon

The moon phase drives the tide. Full moon and new moon bring spring tides with the most energy.

2

Engage the Millstone

Tap the millstone to start grinding. The moon must be at least one-third full or the mill auto-disengages.

3

Manage Wheat & Flour

Watch your wheat supply and flour storage. Sell flour to free up capacity and earn gold.

4

Upgrade Equipment

Spend gold to upgrade the water wheel, millstone, and storage for faster, more efficient milling.

5

Reach the Goal

Hit the flour or gold target before the day limit to complete the contract and earn stars.

6

Progress Through Eras

50 contracts span Stone, Porcelain, Steel, and Ceramic millstone eras with rising difficulty.
