Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Mill By Moonlight is an offline single-player simulation game. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Mill By Moonlight does not collect personal information. The app does not include account registration, social login, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party purchase services.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, unlocked levels, star ratings, and sound settings locally on your device using NSUserDefaults. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Children's Privacy

Mill By Moonlight is suitable for all ages. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Internet, Ads, and Purchases

  • No internet connection is required for gameplay.
  • No advertisements are included.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No user tracking is included.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.

Contact

After release, you can contact support through the Mill By Moonlight App Store product page.
